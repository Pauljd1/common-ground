Our Team

Meet the passionate individuals working to connect people and land through regenerative action and community building.

Founding Team

Sarah Johnson

Co-Founder & Director

Sarah brings 15 years of experience in sustainable agriculture and community organizing. She holds a degree in Environmental Science and has worked with farming cooperatives across three continents. Her passion for regenerative practices stems from witnessing firsthand how healthy soil can transform both landscapes and communities.

Marcus Chen

Co-Founder & Operations Lead

Marcus combines his background in permaculture design with expertise in project management and community development. After leaving his corporate career, he spent five years learning regenerative farming techniques and building intentional communities. He's passionate about creating systems that support both people and planet.

Elena Rodriguez

Community Engagement Coordinator

Elena specializes in building inclusive communities and facilitating meaningful connections between diverse groups of people. With a background in social work and environmental education, she's dedicated to ensuring that regenerative practices are accessible to everyone, regardless of their starting point or resources.

David Thompson

Land Stewardship Advisor

David is a soil scientist and regenerative agriculture consultant with over 20 years of experience helping farmers transition to sustainable practices. He's published research on carbon sequestration and biodiversity restoration, and serves as our technical advisor on land management and regenerative techniques.

Amara Okafor

Education & Outreach Director

Amara develops educational programs and workshops that make regenerative practices accessible to diverse audiences. With a background in adult education and environmental communication, she creates learning experiences that inspire action and build practical skills for sustainable living.
