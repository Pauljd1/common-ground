Our Team

Meet the dedicated researchers and scientists working to unlock hemp's potential for soil remediation and agricultural regeneration.

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Principal Investigator & Director

Dr. Johnson brings 15 years of experience in soil science and phytoremediation research. She holds a PhD in Environmental Science and has published extensively on PFAS contamination and plant-based remediation strategies. Her research focuses on understanding the mechanisms of contaminant uptake in hemp and developing standardized protocols for soil restoration.

Dr. Marcus Chen

Research Coordinator & Field Operations Lead

Dr. Chen combines his background in agricultural engineering with expertise in field trial design and data management. He holds a PhD in Plant Sciences and specializes in hemp cultivation protocols and variety screening. His work focuses on optimizing growing conditions for maximum PFAS uptake and developing scalable remediation practices.

Dr. Elena Rodriguez

Laboratory Director & Analytical Chemist

Dr. Rodriguez specializes in environmental analytical chemistry with a focus on persistent organic pollutants. She holds a PhD in Chemistry and has developed novel methods for detecting and quantifying PFAS compounds in plant tissues. Her work is crucial for understanding how hemp processes and stores contaminants during remediation.

Dr. David Thompson

Soil Health Specialist

Dr. Thompson is a soil scientist with over 20 years of experience in soil health assessment and restoration. He holds a PhD in Soil Science and specializes in measuring the impacts of hemp cultivation on soil carbon, microbial communities, and water retention. His research quantifies the co-benefits of hemp-based remediation for agricultural productivity.

Dr. Amara Okafor

Genetics & Seed Bank Curator

Dr. Okafor develops and maintains our open-source hemp germplasm repository. With a background in plant genetics and conservation biology, she ensures genetic diversity is preserved while identifying high-performing varieties for remediation applications. Her work focuses on sustainable seed production and distribution protocols.
